    About Me

    Introduction

    Josh Romisher is a former Chicago chef, currently in the midst of changing careers to the computer science and software development industry. After over ten years working in the culinary industry, COVID hit the world like a storm and severely impacted the culinary world. This prompted his desire to change careers. He moved back to Ohio to attend Kent State University, and is in his last semester currently. He will be starting a job as a software developer with RoviSys upon graduating in May.

    Education

    Josh attended The Ohio State University after gradutaing high school in 2009 and graduated with a degree in Communications and New Media Technology. Upon graduating from OSU, he decided to pursue a degree in Culinary Arts to further explore his passion for cooking. He joined Kendall College's Culinary Arts program in Chicago, where he continued his education. Josh now attends Kent State University in their Computer Science Bachelor of Science program. He will be graduating this Spring 2024.

    Work

    An aspiring software developer, Josh began his career journey this past summer with an internship at RoviSys. Josh worked as a part of a project team in the Industrial Artificial Intelligence department. He gained valuable career experience working directly with a team on a client project. The project included working with frameworks such as .NET Core, Entity Framework, Azure DevOps and Angular, along with other software development tools like LINQ and Figma. He and his team used the Agile methodology and Scrum as a part of the development process for the project.

    Hobbies

    In his free time, Josh enjoys playing video games in his spare time, along with playing guitar and working on front end programming an app design. He recently got into iOS application development and had a lot of fun designing his first two applications in Swift. Josh is an avid gamer, both PC and console, and enjoys MMOs, RPGs and RTS games. His favorites are The Elder Scrolls Online and Crusader Kings 3. Josh has been playing guitar for 20 years now, and has written a few songs of his own. He played in a band in college and now plays guitar to pass the time on weekends and relieve stress.
